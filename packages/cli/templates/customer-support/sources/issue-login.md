# Support Intent: Authentication / Login Issues

**Description:**
Issues related to customers being unable to log into their accounts, either due to forgotten passwords, locked accounts after failed attempts, or system outages.

**Common Resolutions:**
- Send password reset link.
- Manually unlock account (requires identity verification).
- Escalate to Tier 2 if reset links are failing.
