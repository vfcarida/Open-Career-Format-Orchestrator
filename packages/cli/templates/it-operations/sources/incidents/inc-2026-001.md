# INC-2026-001 — Payment Service Outage

## Impact Summary

- **Duration**: 65 minutes
- **Scope**: 100% of payment transactions failed during the incident window
- **User Impact**: Estimated 14,000 failed transactions. No data loss; all transactions were safely rolled back.
- **SLO Impact**: 65 minutes of downtime burned the entire 30-day error budget.

## What Worked Well

- AKCP agent immediately identified the DB connection pool exhaustion from logs.
- The approval flow ensured the rollback was authorized before execution.
- Audit trail captured full decision chain for postmortem review.
