# Billing and Invoicing FAQ

## How do I update my payment method?
Customers can update their payment method by logging into the portal and navigating to **Settings -> Billing**. As a support agent, you **cannot** update payment methods on their behalf for PCI compliance reasons. Always direct them to the self-service portal.

## What happens if a payment fails?
We retry failed payments automatically 3 times over 7 days. If the final retry fails, the subscription is suspended. 

## Do you offer invoices?
Yes, invoices are automatically emailed to the billing address on file on the 1st of every month. Customers can also download historical invoices from the portal.
